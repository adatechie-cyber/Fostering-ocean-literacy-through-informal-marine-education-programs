About the Conference

This international conference brings together marine scientists, educators, policymakers, and practitioners to explore innovative approaches to ocean literacy through informal education programs.

We'll discuss cutting-edge research, share best practices, and develop collaborative strategies to enhance public understanding of our oceans and promote sustainable marine conservation efforts worldwide.

The conference will feature keynote presentations, interactive workshops, poster sessions, and field excursions to marine education centers.
